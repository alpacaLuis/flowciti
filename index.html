Can my university use flowciti ?

Yes! We’re currently seeking university partners that will use our web app for a minimal fee in exchange of feedback. One of our goal is to discourage the use of tracing paper while still being able to further develop your ideas. Get in touch with us!

What are the minimum specs to run flowciti ?

Unlike many computer programs for urban planners, flowciti does not require a powerful configuration to run. Our web app is cloud based which only requires a modern browser. Optimization is within our main priorities :)

Is flowciti expensive ?

No, quite the contrary. This is a prototyping and modeling tool that doesn’t use expensive proprietary data which leads to a high set-up cost. Our pricing model consists of a LOW monthly subscription fee per user that lets us achieve our growth goals.

Where can I find an investor package ?

Unfortunately, we are not looking for investors. We are however looking for partners. Our ideal partners have a strong network of contacts with existing government, transportation, and advocacy agency relationships.  It’s not about putting your logo on our app. It’s about making our cities better places.
